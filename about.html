About Rock Your Dream

The Rock Your Dream Chair is a collaborative intervention with Tim van den Burg, a creative maker from Breda, the Urban Living Lab Breda, and THE SPACE, which aims to foster a sense of community in urban environments.

THE SPACE is a platform to bring artistic practice into academic research founded by Marisa de Brito, Licia Calvi and Laura Pardo at the Breda University of Applied Sciences which operates under the premises of the theme Places & Flows within the Research & Graduate School of the university.
