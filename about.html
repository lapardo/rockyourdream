About Rock Your Dream

The Rock Your Dream Chair is a collaborative intervention with Tim van den Burg, a creative maker from Breda, the Urban Living Lab Breda, and THE SPACE, which aims to foster a sense of community in urban environments.

THE SPACE is a platform to bring artistic practice into academic research innitiated by Marisa de Brito, Licia Calvi and Laura Pardo at Breda University of Applied Sciences powered by the theme Places & Flows within the Research & Graduate School.
